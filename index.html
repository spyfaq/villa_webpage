Nearby Beaches

Beautiful beaches near the villa

Some of Corfu’s most beautiful beaches are just minutes away.

Kontokali Beach

Just a few minutes away, this organized beach offers calm waters, beach bars and restaurants.

Dassia Beach

A popular beach known for its crystal-clear waters, water sports and vibrant seaside tavernas.

Gouvia Beach

A charming beach close to Gouvia Marina with beautiful sunset views and seaside cafés.

Explore Corfu

Things to do during your stay

Corfu Old Town

Just 6 km away, explore UNESCO-listed Corfu Town with Venetian architecture, charming alleys and historic landmarks.

Gouvia Marina

One of the largest marinas in the Ionian Sea, offering restaurants, cafés and beautiful waterfront views.

Boat Trips

Discover the Ionian Sea with private boat tours to nearby islands, hidden beaches and sea caves.
